Who we are

We’re a hands-on electronics repair and digital solutions studio in Kikuyu, Kenya — built to keep people connected and businesses running.

Founded in 2023, Bobby Tech started as a repair bench and a bold idea: make tech support fast, fair, and friendly. Today we serve individuals, SMEs, and institutions with repairs, IT support, and digital services — always backed by craftsmanship and heart.
